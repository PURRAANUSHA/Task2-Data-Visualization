# Insights from Car Price Analysis

## Key Findings

1. High-end brands like BMW, Audi, and Mercedes have higher average selling prices.
2. Automatic transmission cars are more common than manual ones.
3. Car prices generally increase with newer manufacturing years.
4. SUVs and Sedans dominate the dataset and have higher average prices.
5. Some brands have a high number of listings, showing market popularity.
6. There is a wide price variation across different body types.
7. Cheapest cars are mostly older models with higher mileage.

The dataset shows clear trends in pricing based on brand, year, and car type.
